Teishoku Ochazuke Balls

Description

A frozen riceball that you can easily dissolve in broth or soup for a quick breakfast.

Ingredients

  • protein of choice: salmon, chicken
  • rice
  • sesame seeds
  • dried toppings: seaweed, green onion, baby shrimp, woodear mushroom, etc.
  • broth
  • tools: scale, plastic wrap

Steps

  1. lay down plastic wrap
  2. split items into even and reasonably sized portions, using the scale for consistency
  3. wrap and shape into balls, then freeze
  4. when eating -- microwave for a few minutes, then add to a bowl with heated-up broth
