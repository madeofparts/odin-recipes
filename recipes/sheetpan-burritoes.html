Sheetpan Breakfast Burritoes

Description

Easy microwave and freezer-friendly sheetpan burritoes for a quick start to a slow morning.

Ingredients

For Sheet Pan Filling

  • 2 yukon gold potatoes
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1/2 small yellow onion
  • olive oil
  • salt to taste
  • garlic, onion, paprika, chili powder -- 1/4tsp
  • dried oregano 1/4 tsp
  • 16 oz ground italian sausage

For Eggs

  • 12 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup water
  • salt to taste
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder

For Assembly

  • 12 tortillas
  • shredded cheese
  • chipotle sauce

Steps

  1. Roast veggies at 400F for 15 minutes, tossed in olive oil and seasonings.
  2. Add the sausage, roast for 15 more minutes.
  3. Pour eggs over sheet pan, and bake for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Cut the eggs into 12 pieces, add cheese and sauce to each tortilla.
  5. Roll tightly and cook for 2-3 minutes a side.
