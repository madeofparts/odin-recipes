Ricecooker Pepper Lunch

Description

Trendy ricecooker-only meal including decent protein and fresh vegg.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz seasoned marinated chicken
  • 3/4 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 medium onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup corn, frozen
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 scallion, chopped
  • sauce: 1 soy - 1 dark soy - 0.5 hoisin - 1 honey + black pepper & garlic powder to taste

Steps

  1. put them all in rice cooker, except for butter, scallion, sauce.
  2. once cooked, add remaining ingredients.
